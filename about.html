Hum Style Awards
2022

The Hum Awards are an annual awards show that celebrates the best in Pakistani cinema. The award show was first held in 2013, and since then, it has become one of the most anticipated events in the Pakistani entertainment calendar. The Hum Awards 2022 will take place in Toronto, Canada on Saturday, 24th of September 2022, in conjunction with Firstontario Center Hamilton. Only a few days remain before the 15th of September 2022 to vote for your favorite celebrity.

HUM Awards is amongst the top-rated awards shows in the South Asian sub-continent. for sponsership inquiries please contact
